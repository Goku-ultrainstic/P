ABOUT

Billie Eilish (born December 18, 2001, Los Angeles, California, U.S.) first gained recognition in 2015 for the song "Ocean Eyes"and became, in 2020, the youngest person ever to win a Grammy for the album of the year, for When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?Her other works include Happier Than Ever (2021).
